Kaoru Sumi, Dr. Eng.

Kaoru Sumi is CRL Fellow of Breakthrough21 Nishida Project
in Kansai Advanced Research Center, Communications Research Laboratory
Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications

Her research interest covers Knowledge Engineering, Communication Support Systems, User Modeling, and Human Interface.

She is a member of Japan Association for Artificial Intelligence (JSAI), Information Processing Society of Japan (IPSJ), and Association for Computing Machinery (ACM).
