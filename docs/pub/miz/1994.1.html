Title: Current state of the art of knowledge sharing and reuse
Author: Riichiro Mizoguchi
Reference: J. of JSAI, Vol.9, No.1, pp.3-9, 1994

Abstract:

Knowledge sharing and reuse have been gathering much attention in AI community. The technology is expected to make a break-through of the current shortcomings of the current knowledge processing technology, since many of the drawbacks of knowledge-based technology are because of lack of knowledge reuse and sharing. This paper discusses the basic issues of knowledge sharing and reuse research and describes its current state of the art together with some activites in Japan.
